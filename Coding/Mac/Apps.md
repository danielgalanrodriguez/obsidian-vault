# Recut
#video #editing
Automatically removes silence from the videos.
Detects the silent parts, trims it from the video. 
Use the silence to separate the different takes.
Now you have all your takes split in different sections that can be easily spotted in Imovie.

brew: https://formulae.brew.sh/cask/recut
src: https://getrecut.com/?ref=fromsergio
